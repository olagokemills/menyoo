The Story of Menyoo



Menyoo is a visual database of service-led restaurant menus, accessed via the menyoo mobile. The goal of menyoo is to enagage the global dinning community by acting as a centralised, accessible catalogue of visual restaurant menus.

We partner with the best restaurants in the business and give you access to meals you love, when you want it.

Menyoo operates in the food service/e-commerce trade sector.

At Menyoo, our goal is to help you create lasting momories by connecting you to good food you truly love as fast as possible.

Whether you choose to dine solo or enjoy the company of family or friends, Menyoo has got you. You can find diet specific menus such as vegan, vegetarian, ketogenic or diary-free meals from our diverse restaurant partners all over he world. Every meal caters to just you, fresh and fast as you like it.

Our Customers




Whether you are a party of 10 or 2, excite your tastebuds by accessing a wide variety of restaurant menus anywhere you are.

Our goal at Menyoo is to connect people, food and technology together. Our curated virtual catalogue of global menus will help you tell you own dining story the way you want it.
